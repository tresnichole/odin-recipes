Lasgna baby

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 (10 ounce) can enchilada sauce
  • 10 fluid ounces milk
  • 1 (13 ounce) package flavored tortilla snack chips (doritios)

Directions

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step 2 Cook and stir ground beef in a large pot over medium-high heat until the beef is crumbly and completely browned, 7 to 10 minutes. Drain and discard excess grease.
  3. Step 3 Stir cream of chicken soup, enchilada sauce, and milk with the ground beef; bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4 Spread crushed tortilla snack chips into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Pour ground beef mixture over crushed chips; stir. Arrange cheese slices atop the mixture.
  5. Step 5 Bake in preheated oven until the cheese melts, about 5 minutes; stir the melted cheese into the beef mixture.
